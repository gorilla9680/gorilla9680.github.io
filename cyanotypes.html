Cyanotype Photography
My experimental work with 19th century photographic processes. - Cyanotype
In the second year of my undergraduate study, I received a full scholarship to attend a course on 19th century photographic processes in Santa Fe Univeristy of Art and Design in New Mexico, USA for a month.

As a part of the coursework I was encouraged to experiment with photographs made using the cyanotype process on varying kinds of papers as well as the ‘Silver Gelatin’ process on a medium format camera.

I was awarded ‘Best in Show’ following the showcase of our work at the end of the course.
